About Us

Flexible Builders Ltd is a locally owned and operated construction company based in New Zealand. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we’ve built our reputation on reliability, craftsmanship, and clear communication. Whether you’re planning a small home renovation or a full-scale new build, we bring the same level of care, quality, and expertise to every job.

Our team works closely with homeowners, landlords, and developers to deliver results that are both beautiful and built to last. We’re fully licensed and insured, and we take pride in completing projects on time and within budget. No job is too big or too small — from fences and decks to kitchens, bathrooms, and complete home transformations.
